About Us

Information
Fashion house Fashionist was established in 1970 and until this day remains as one of the oldest apparel manufacturer and fashion retailers in georgia. Recently the brand gave birth to a premium line Fashionist. The brand is unique in its approach to the fashion business. collections here are presented by the talented Georgian designers who all work under Fashionist. The line aids the evolution of fashion industry by giving opportunities to young and emerging designers to expand their possibilities and talent. Currently Fashionist's designer team is lead by Lara Smith and Done Jone Crysler.
